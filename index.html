About Eureka

Eureka is an app that engages individuals and encourages them to make a big contribution in reducing the over all greenhouse emissions. Our quiz provides you with personalized insights into your carbon footprint and empowers you to take action to reduce it. We also offer a rewards program that allows you to earn points for making sustainable choices.

How to Eureka works:

1. Calculates the greenhouse emissions of an individual by asking a few simple questions

2. Helps you find other green living enthusiasts online

3. Creates an elaborate ranking system (in your neighbourhood), ranking citizens based on their monthly emissions, regularly notifying you to reduce your emissions

4. It awards you with badges on decreasing your monthly emissions!
